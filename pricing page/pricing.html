    FAQ

    • Is “Basic Plan” available only for a limited time?

      Nope. It is free-forever and there is no credit-card required to sign-up and to start using Mini Course Generator with features included in the “Basic Plan”.

    • Is refund possible if my credit card is charged after I move to paid plans?

      Yes. Please let us know through team@minicoursegenerator.com within 14 days after your credit card is charged, and we will instantly refund 100% of your money. No questions asked.

    • Why AI-powered mini course generation is limited in paid plans?

      We are using artificial intelligence (GPT-3 & GPT-4) for automatic mini-course generation, and there is a cost associated with each content generation process every time. For that reason, there is a course generation limit for each month.

    • Do you charge any fees from the sales we make?

      Nope. We do not charge any fees nor get a commission from your sales. The only cut will be Stripe’s transaction fee. These fees are charged by the payment processor and are not associated with us.

    • Do you offer discounts for charities, NGOs and educational institutions?

      Yes. Please get in touch with us through team@minicoursegenerator.com and we will help you about that.
